Mission

The word Yantra in Sanskrit means instrument — a tool built with intention, aligned with a deeper purpose. Human is the reminder of who it serves.

HumanYantra was born from a simple observation: the AI revolution is happening fast, and most people are watching from the outside. The benefits are concentrating. The complexity is intimidating. The ethics are an afterthought.

We believe there is something worth holding onto in this moment — a memory of putting human flourishing at the centre of knowledge, not productivity. Of asking for what? before asking how fast?

Our mission is to build and make available AI tools grounded in that question — tools that help real people with real problems. And to do it with conscience, clarity, and care for the people being left behind.
